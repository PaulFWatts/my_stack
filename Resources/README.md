## Learning Resources

### HTML

[HTML elements reference - HTML | MDN](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/HTML/Reference/Elements)

**Here’s a tight, practical list of the 10 most useful modern HTML tags & attributes you’ll actually use heavily with HTMX:**

### Top Modern Tags

1. **<main>** – The main content area of your page. Great root target for HTMX swaps.
2. **<section>** – Thematic grouping of content. Perfect for partial page updates.
3. **<article>** – Self-contained content (cards, posts, items).
4. **<dialog>** – Native modal/dialog. HTMX makes opening/closing these trivial.
5.  **<details> + <summary>** – Built-in accordion. No JS needed until you add HTMX enhancements.

### Most Useful Attributes (especially with HTMX)

1. **id** & **class** – Your primary HTMX targeting tools (hx-target="#results" or .item).
2. **name** (on inputs) – Critical for forms that HTMX posts.
3. **value** – Works beautifully with HTMX for dynamic values.
4. **for** (on <label>) – Makes forms accessible and click-friendly on mobile.
5. **Modern input attributes**:
   - type="search", type="email", type="tel"
   - required, autocomplete, pattern, autofocus

### Bonus Quick Tips for HTMX

- Use hx-target="closest section" or hx-target="#main" a lot.
- <button> and <form> are your best friends.
- <input list="…"> + <datalist> for nice autocomplete without extra code.
